Immortal Balkan Esports is a professional esport team based in Croatia, funded in December 2019, with the intention of spreading positive influences and benefits of professional esports activities. We go against prejudice of professional gaming being negative activity while enabling conditions for talented players for further development and personal growth in Esports world. We encourage healthy competitive environment, without negativities and erase physical boundaries between countries, nationalities and religions. We gladly cooperate with other teams, organizations and companies with similar area of interest in further development and improvement of gaming industry.The area of our work is not geographically limited; we are recognizable in Europe and USA mainly because of top plays provided by our roster team. Current roster counts 6 professional Fortnite players with excellent results on competitions in Balkan, European and USA tournaments. We are developing competitive teams in other games with a wish to secure our spot in this extremely popular branch of competitive Esport. We are also very successful in organizing Balkan based tournaments that were very popular in gaming community and very well received. Our community now counts respectable number of members on platforms such as Discord, Instagram, Twitter, Trovo and Youtube with a cooperation with more than dozen popular Content Creators and gaming influencers. Team manager and founders of this organnization are IB DarkShade and IB Softly with an extensive help of pro staff, community managers and gaming community without which all idea would be worthless!

So join us and become a part of the most sucessful gaming community!
